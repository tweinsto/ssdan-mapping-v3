What do these percentages mean?
  • % Disabled (65+): Share of adults 65 and over who report any disability, including difficulties with hearing, vision, cognition, walking, self-care, or independent living. (ACS Table C18130)
  • % in Poverty (65+): Adults 65+ whose income is below the federal poverty threshold, which varies by household size and composition. (ACS Table B17001)
  • % Not Insured (65+): Adults 65+ without any health insurance coverage, including Medicare or other public/private plans. (ACS Table B27010)

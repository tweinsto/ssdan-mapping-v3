What Do These Housing Indicators Show? All values are based on renter-occupied households in each county.
  • % Renters: Share of households living in rented housing (Table B25003 – Tenure).
  • % Rent Burdened (30%+): Renters spending 30%+ of income on rent (Table B25070 – Gross Rent as a Percentage of Household Income in the Past 12 Months).
  • Rental Vacancy Rate: Share of rental units that are currently vacant (Table B25004 – Vacancy Status).
  • Median Gross Rent: Monthly rent including utilities (if paid by tenant) (Table B25064 – Median Gross Rent).
